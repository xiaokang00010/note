Unit2 My favorite season A Let's Learn

一年四季

Spring :

  • n. 春天；春季
  • n. 泉水
  • n. 弹簧；发条
  • v. 跳

春天的特点 :

  • It rains a lot
  • Birds sing songs
  • The weather is warm
  • Children have fun.
  • Some trees are blooming (开花)
  • The grass is growing.
  • Beautiful butter

Summer :

  • n. 夏天；夏季

夏天的特点 ：

  • people wear swimsuits (泳衣)
  • Riding on a jet ski (摩托艇)
  • travel on an island (岛)
  • sit under a beach umbrella
  • eat an ice cream
  • build sandcastles (堆城堡)

Autumn :

  • n. 秋天；秋季
  • 形近词 : fall

秋天的特点 :

  • Weather gets colder
  • is a harvest season
  • Wind is blowing
  • leaves (树叶) are falling
  • everything color is gold

Winter :

  • n. 冬天；冬季

冬天的特点 :

  • days are shorter
  • Everything is covered in snow
  • The water is frozen
  • make snowballs
  • make a snowman
  • go ice-skating
